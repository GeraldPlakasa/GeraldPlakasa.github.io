Analysis Cookie Cats Game

Project information

  • Category: Analysis
  • Project date: June 2022
  • Project URL: Power Point Project

     Within the realm of mobile gaming, Cookie Cats, a highly renowned puzzle game developed by Tactile Entertainment, takes center stage. In this portfolio entry, a meticulous and all-encompassing analysis unfolds, aimed at investigating the repercussions of relocating the game's initial gate from level 30 to the more demanding level 40. The primary focus revolves around the examination of two critical facets: player retention and gameplay rounds.

     Upon analyzing the results, it becomes apparent that the gate relocation experiment has a discernible effect on player engagement. Within a single day, there is a notable one percent decrease in player retention, which further deepens to a substantial four percent decline after the span of seven days. These findings underscore the crucial role that strategic game progression adjustments play in shaping player behavior and overall gaming experiences.
