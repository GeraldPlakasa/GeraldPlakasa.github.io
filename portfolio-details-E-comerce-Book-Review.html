     The portfolio entails a comprehensive analysis of book sales within an e-commerce platform, specifically catering to book enthusiasts. The analysis primarily focuses on providing valuable insights to book lovers by showcasing the average ratings and number of reviews for books published in each respective year. This information enables book lovers to make informed decisions about whether to read or purchase books based on their publication year.

     Furthermore, the analysis includes a chart that displays the authors who have received reviews. This allows book lovers to identify trending authors, as well as those who have written highly regarded or even poorly received books. Additionally, book lovers can also assess the average ratings for books authored by each writer, providing them with an additional criterion to consider when making their book selections.

     In essence, this portfolio entry highlights the importance of data-driven analysis in the realm of book sales. By presenting average ratings and reviews for books published in different years, as well as insights into author performance, the analysis empowers book enthusiasts to make informed decisions and discover engaging literary works within their preferred genres.
