Paimon Chatbot

Project information

  • Category: Natural Language Processing
  • Project date: June 2021
  • Project URL: github.com/unsri-hackers/Paimon

     In collaboration with alumni, a notable project was undertaken to develop a Chatbot aimed at enhancing the interactivity of a Telegram group and facilitating the dissemination of up-to-date news related to Technology. This endeavor entailed harnessing the power of the Python programming language along with the Telegram Bot library.

     The project's primary objective was to create a dynamic and engaging environment within the Telegram group by integrating a Chatbot. Through the skillful application of Python, the Chatbot was designed to deliver real-time news updates and provide interactive functionalities, thereby fostering meaningful interactions among group members. The incorporation of the Telegram Bot library further augmented the Chatbot's capabilities, enabling seamless integration and communication within the Telegram platform.

     This portfolio entry showcases the successful implementation of the Chatbot, highlighting its pivotal role in transforming the Telegram group into a vibrant hub of interaction and information sharing. The utilization of Python and the Telegram Bot library underscores the dedication to delivering a robust and user-friendly solution that effectively meets the needs of the group's members.
