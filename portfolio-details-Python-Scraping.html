Python Scraping

Project information

  • Category: Natural Language Processing
  • Project date: February 2021
  • Project URL: github.com/GeraldPlakasa/Python-Scraping

     The portfolio encompasses an in-depth exploration of web scraping techniques for extracting information from the vast expanse of the internet and various websites. The primary focus of this endeavor lies in the meticulous segmentation and subsequent evaluation of different methods, namely Punkt, Split, and Tokenize, to determine the most effective approach.

     The project involves leveraging sophisticated web scraping tools to gather pertinent information from diverse online sources. Subsequently, the collected data is subjected to careful segmentation, wherein the effectiveness of the Punkt, Split, and Tokenize methods is assessed. By employing systematic evaluation criteria, this portfolio entry aims to ascertain the optimal method for achieving accurate and reliable segmentation of the scraped data.

     This analysis serves as a testament to the value of web scraping as a means of extracting relevant information from the internet. By scrutinizing the merits of different segmentation methods, the project sheds light on the intricate nuances and challenges involved in information extraction and organization.
