Rule Based Topic Detection

Project information

  • Category: Natural Language Processing
  • Project date: August 2022
  • Project URL: github.com/GeraldPlakasa/rule-based-topic-detection

     The Topic Detection application is an Excel VBA-based tool designed to analyze customer sentiments and comments regarding products or businesses. This sophisticated application comprises several key processes, including comment translation from Indonesian to English, keyword extraction, comment categorization, location extraction, and sentiment analysis.

     This portfolio entry highlights the development of the Topic Detection application, showcasing its versatility in processing and analyzing customer sentiments. By leveraging Excel VBA, the application offers a powerful and user-friendly platform for handling and interpreting comments.

     The application's primary functionalities include the translation of comments from Indonesian to English, the extraction of keywords, categorization of comments, extraction of location information, and sentiment analysis. These processes collectively enable businesses to gain valuable insights into customer opinions and sentiments, allowing them to make data-driven decisions and enhance their products or services accordingly.
